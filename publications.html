Standards

IEEE P7021

Standard for Ethical AI in Climate Projections.

Nilantha co-created the Project Authorization Request (PAR) for IEEE P7021, approved in December 2025, and serves as Co-Lead and Secretary of the working group. The standard sets out ethical guardrails for how AI systems are used to generate and communicate climate projections - directly informed by the institutional adoption research at the centre of his PhD.

Dr Mahendra Samarawickrama serves as Working Group Chair, reflecting IEEE membership requirements for the role.
