Volunteer & Community

Community work in Sri Lanka running alongside the research and teaching, from founding organisations to hands-on fundraising and school support.

Organisations

Founded and co-founded

2018 - Present

Founder, Shaping Heroes Foundation

A social development organisation focused on educational capacity-building for Sri Lankan schoolchildren, aiming to strengthen leadership and character alongside academic support.

2017 - Present

Co-Founder & Chief Planning and Fundraising Officer, Sahodarathwaye Manudama (Brotherhood for Humanity)

A social support programme established to assist people who need help physically or mentally. Sahodarathwaye Manudama is the organisation's name in Sinhala; Brotherhood for Humanity is its English name.

On the Ground

Selected volunteer work

2018 & 2019

Sri Pada pilgrimage for the blind

Led a group of blind pilgrims on the walk from Moratuwa to Sri Pada (Adam's Peak).

Ongoing

Tree-planting campaigns

Wilpattu, Kalpitiya, Hawagala, and Bolgoda Lake, alongside several partner organisations.

Ongoing

Rural school support

Donated stationery and equipment, and ran motivational programmes, across roughly ten rural schools. Founded the "Mission" and "Deeptha" project series to support under-resourced schools, and developed computer laboratories for rural communities in Kohonawa, Madolsima, and Ohiya.

Ongoing

Healthcare fundraising

Took part in a fundraising walk for Apeksha Cancer Hospital, Maharagama, and contributed to donating roughly Rs. 600,000 in essential medicines to the hospital.

Since 2020

Forest monasteries

Helped build forest monasteries at Memure, Kalugala, Pulmudai, and Sewanagala.
